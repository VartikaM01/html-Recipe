Process:

  1. Heat oil in a thick bottomed pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot. Add sliced onions and whole peeled garlic
  3. Now turn the flame to low and let the onion and garlic caramelise.
  4. Once they have caramelised, let it cool down.
  5. Now in a blender add coconut milk,caramelised onions and garlic,herbs,peppercorns,salt.Blend away!
  6. Boil pasta of your choice in the side. I prefer spaghetti.
  7. Now remove the pasta from the boiling water. Add the blended sauce. Add some pasta water if needed.
  8. Viola!It's ready!!!
