Ingredients

(Serves 2 people)

  • 2 large onions
    • thinly slice the onions
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 10-12 crushed black peppercorns
  • Heavy cream or coconut milk(Honestly I prefer coconut milk)
  • dried herbs of your choice
  • a handful dryfruits of your choice
    • I prefer a combination of cashews, peanuts and pistachios
  • salt to taste
